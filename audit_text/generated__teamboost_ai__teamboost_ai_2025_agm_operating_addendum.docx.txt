Operating addendum (Exhibit B)
TeamBoost.ai, Inc.
*(Delaware Corporation)*

Purpose
This addendum supplements the Minutes of the Annual Meeting of the Board of Directors of the Corporation for 2025 and is annexed to those minutes as Exhibit B.

Detailed accomplishments (2025)

- Released the first private version via TestFlight and coordinated initial App Store launch
- Continued improving the mobile and web applications, enhancing core features and user experience
- Expanded automated test coverage across the platform
- Enhanced onboarding, organization management, and notifications services
- Extended backend services (DMS, reminders, EWS, requests/questions, comments, attachments) with new features
- Improved system stability and performance
- Enhanced the tasks service and related workflows
- Implemented automated tests for task-related functionality
- Introduced new web application design and continued feature development
