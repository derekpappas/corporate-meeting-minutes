Immediately following the Annual Meeting of Stockholders of the Corporation held on 2026-12-16 commencing at 1:00 PM, The Annual Meeting of the Board of Directors of DATA RECORD SCIENCE, INC. III. Roll Call and Quorum
Director Present:

None

With Derek E. Pappas present as the Sole Director, the Board had a quorum and lawfully convened to transact business under the Delaware General Corporation Law.

The Sole Director joined the meeting by approved digital means, with audio contemporaneous among participants, and such attendance was recorded as present in person at the meeting for quorum purposes.

IV. Approval of Prior Minutes
The minutes of the prior Annual Meeting of the Board of Directors held on 2025-12-10 were reviewed and approved by the Sole Director.
V. Reports of Officers

President’s Report:
The President reported that the Corporation is operated principally as a Delaware patent holding company, holding patents and related rights without conducting commercial software engineering as an operating business. Corporate administration, outside counsel coordination, and portfolio maintenance were overseen from Wayne, Pennsylvania, USA; the Corporation did not maintain third-party product-development centers comparable to a commercial SaaS operator during the year. The Sole Director summarized patent annuity and prosecution updates, portfolio housekeeping, and counsel reporting for the year.  Patents, patent applications, and related intellectual property held for investment were reaffirmed as assets of the Corporation.

Treasurer’s Report:
The Treasurer reported that the Corporation remains solvent and that certain outstanding obligations, including legacy acquisition-related holdbacks and similar obligations that remain contingent on a future liquidity event, the timing of which has not yet been determined. The Sole Director acknowledged the status of such obligations and confirmed continued oversight of these matters. Franchise taxes and registered agent fees are paid and current. The Corporation has 5,346,132 shares of common stock issued and outstanding at a par value of $0.001 per share.

For the actions described herein, the Sole Director relied in good faith on materials and oral presentations furnished for the meeting—including financial and operating summaries from officers of the Corporation—and on other information, reports, and statements presented as to matters within the presenters’ professional or expert competence, within the meaning of Section 141(e) of the Delaware General Corporation Law.


VI. Discussion Items
The Sole Director discussed portfolio strategy for 2027, including claim scope reviews, continuation strategy, and coordination with outside patent counsel.

VII. Resolutions
Upon consideration, the Sole Director adopted the following resolutions:

Approval of Financial Reports
RESOLVED, that the financial statements for the fiscal year 2026 are hereby approved.

Approval of 2027 Budget
RESOLVED, that the operating, engineering, and marketing budget for the fiscal year 2027 is hereby approved.

Banking Authorization
RESOLVED, that Derek E. Pappas is authorized to open, maintain, and manage one or more corporate bank accounts in the name of the Corporation at Wells Fargo Bank, N.A., and any successor institution, and to act as the sole authorized signatory with full authority to execute all related documents.



VIII. Adjournment
There being no further business to come before the Board, the meeting was adjourned.

Derek E. Pappas
