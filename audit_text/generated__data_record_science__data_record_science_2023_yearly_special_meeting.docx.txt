
Minutes of the Special Meeting of the Board of Directors - 2023
DATA RECORD SCIENCE, INC.
(Board of Directors – DE corporation)

Meeting Details
Date of Meeting: 2023-12-03
Time of Meeting: 12:00 PM
Location of Meeting: Thessaloniki, Greece, via digital communication
Address note: The principal address above is the Corporation’s designated notice and filing address on the corporate records. The Corporation is governed by the Delaware General Corporation Law; statutory references in these minutes follow that domicile. Place (and, where applicable, remote participation) reflects where the meeting was conducted for the stated date and may differ from the principal address.
Purpose: Pre-annual review of corporate records, patent portfolio status, and registered-agent compliance

I. Call to Order:
The Special Meeting of the Board of Directors of DATA RECORD SCIENCE, INC. (the “Corporation”) was called to order at 12:00 PM on 2023-12-03 by Derek E. Pappas, acting as Sole Director of the Corporation.

II. Roll Call and Quorum:
Director Present: Derek E. Pappas (Sole Director)
Director Absent: None

The Sole Director being present, a quorum was present, and the meeting was duly constituted to transact business in accordance with the Delaware General Corporation Law.
The Sole Director confirmed that notice had been duly given or validly waived for this meeting.
The Sole Director joined the meeting by approved digital means, with audio contemporaneous among participants, and such attendance was recorded as present in person at the meeting for quorum purposes.

III. Resolutions:
Upon consideration, the Sole Director adopted the following resolutions:

Ratification of Corporate and Patent-Portfolio Administration
RESOLVED, that corporate housekeeping, outside-counsel coordination, and patent-portfolio maintenance decisions affecting the Corporation during 2023 are hereby ratified, confirmed, and approved in all respects.

Record Date for Annual Meeting of Stockholders (DGCL §213)
RESOLVED, that 2023-12-03 is hereby fixed as the record date for determining the stockholders entitled to notice of and to vote at the Annual Meeting of Stockholders of the Corporation to be held on 2023-12-13 commencing at 1:00 PM, in accordance with the Corporation’s bylaws and the Delaware General Corporation Law.


The Sole Director recorded reliance, in good faith, on the financial and operational materials circulated or reviewed for this meeting and on statements from officers of the Corporation on matters reasonably treated as within such persons’ professional or expert competence, as permitted by Section 141(e) of the Delaware General Corporation Law.


IV. Adjournment:
There being no further business to come before the Board, the meeting was adjourned.

Derek E. Pappas
