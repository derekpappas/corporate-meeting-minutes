
Minutes of the Special Meeting of the Board of Directors - 2024
Ritual Growth, Inc.
(Board of Directors – DE corporation)

Meeting Details
Date of Meeting: 2024-12-10
Time of Meeting: 12:00 PM
Location of Meeting: Denver, NC, via digital communication
Address note: The principal address above is the Corporation’s designated notice and filing address on the corporate records. Purpose: Pre-annual review of product delivery, hosting footprint, and partner commitments

I. Call to Order:
The Special Meeting of the Board of Directors of Ritual Growth, Inc. (the “Corporation”) was called to order at 12:00 PM on 2024-12-10 by Derek E. Pappas, acting as Sole Director of the Corporation.

II. Roll Call and Quorum:
Director Present: Derek E. Pappas (Sole Director)

The Sole Director’s presence satisfied the quorum requirement, and the meeting proceeded as duly constituted under the Delaware General Corporation Law. The Sole Director acknowledged on the record that notice requirements were satisfied by proper notice or waiver.
The Sole Director attended using remote communications by which each participant could hear the others, and treated that participation as presence in person where permitted by applicable law and the Corporation’s bylaws.

III. Resolutions:
Upon consideration, the Sole Director adopted the following resolution:

Ratification of Product Delivery and Hosting Operations
RESOLVED, that engineering, hosting, and go-to-market decisions affecting the Corporation’s web applications and API services during 2024 are hereby ratified, confirmed, and approved in all respects.


In taking the actions reflected in these minutes, the Sole Director relied in good faith on information, opinions, reports, and statements—including financial and operational materials prepared for this meeting and presentations from officers of the Corporation—as to matters the Sole Director reasonably believed were within such persons’ professional or expert competence, as contemplated by Section 141(e) of the Delaware General Corporation Law.


IV. Adjournment:
There being no further business to come before the Board, the meeting was adjourned.

Signature:
Derek E. Pappas
Dated: December 10, 2024
