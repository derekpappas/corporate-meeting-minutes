
Notice of Annual Meeting of Stockholders
DATA RECORD SCIENCE, INC.
*(Delaware corporation)*

To the stockholders of the Corporation:

Notice is hereby given that an annual meeting of stockholders of DATA RECORD SCIENCE, INC. (the “Corporation”) will be held:

Date: December 11, 2024 (2024-12-11)
Time: 1:00 PM
Place / means of participation: Denver, NC, via digital communication

Record date: The record date for determining stockholders entitled to notice of and to vote at the meeting (or any adjournment or postponement) is 2024-12-01, as fixed by the Board of Directors in accordance with the bylaws and the Delaware General Corporation Law.

Purpose
To elect directors and to transact such other business as may properly come before the meeting and any adjournment or postponement, in accordance with the certificate of incorporation and bylaws.

Stockholders may attend and vote in person or, where permitted by the bylaws and applicable law, by remote communication in the manner described in this notice or in supplemental instructions provided by the Corporation.

This notice is given pursuant to the Delaware General Corporation Law and the Corporation’s bylaws. Notice may be delivered by hand, United States mail, or electronic transmission in the manner and within the time frames permitted by applicable law and the bylaws.

Principal executive office: 30 N Gould St Ste 24165, Sheridan, WY 82801
