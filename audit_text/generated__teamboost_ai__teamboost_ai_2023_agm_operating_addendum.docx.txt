Operating addendum (Exhibit B)
TeamBoost.ai, Inc.
*(Delaware Corporation)*

Purpose
This addendum supplements the Minutes of the Annual Meeting of the Board of Directors of the Corporation for 2023 and is annexed to those minutes as Exhibit B.

Detailed accomplishments (2023)

- Defined and documented technical specifications for the Teamboost API and mobile application
- Developed the Teamboost mobile and web applications
- Released the mobile application for private beta testing
- Implemented core features including tasks, projects, task groups, and labels
- Developed onboarding, notifications, and organization management services
- Established backend architecture supporting onboarding, real-time notifications, and organizational workflows
- Designed and developed the tasks service enabling core task management functionality

Other materials
To the extent referenced in the minutes, this addendum may also include or incorporate by reference supplemental technical materials furnished for the meeting (including technical specifications, roadmaps, KPIs, and architecture diagrams).
