
Minutes of the Quarterly Governance Meeting - 2023 Q2
DATA RECORD SCIENCE, INC.
(Board of Directors – DE corporation)

Meeting Details
Date of Meeting: 2023-07-03
Time of Meeting: 1:00 PM
Location of Meeting: Wayne, Pennsylvania, via digital communication
Address note: The principal address above is the Corporation’s designated notice and filing address on the corporate records. The Corporation is governed by the Delaware General Corporation Law; statutory references in these minutes follow that domicile. Place (and, where applicable, remote participation) reflects where the meeting was conducted for the stated date and may differ from the principal address.

I. Call to Order:
The Quarterly Governance Meeting of the Board of Directors of DATA RECORD SCIENCE, INC. (the “Corporation”) was called to order at 1:00 PM on 2023-07-03 by Derek E. Pappas, acting as Sole Director of the Corporation.

II. Roll Call and Quorum:
Director Present: Derek E. Pappas (Sole Director)
Director Absent: None

Quorum being established by the attendance of the Sole Director, the meeting was duly organized for business under the Delaware General Corporation Law.
The Sole Director confirmed that notice of the meeting was duly given or waived.
The Sole Director joined the meeting by approved digital means, with audio contemporaneous among participants, and such attendance was recorded as present in person at the meeting for quorum purposes.

III. Business Review:
The Sole Director reviewed quarterly franchise tax, registered agent, and minute-book compliance, and confirmed that the Corporation’s patent portfolio records remained current as reported by counsel. The Corporation did not operate product engineering centers during Q2 2023; intellectual-property assets continued to be held at the parent level.

The Sole Director recorded reliance, in good faith, on the financial and operational materials circulated or reviewed for this meeting and on statements from officers of the Corporation on matters reasonably treated as within such persons’ professional or expert competence, as permitted by Section 141(e) of the Delaware General Corporation Law.


IV. Resolution:
Upon consideration, the Sole Director adopted the following resolution:

RESOLVED, that all corporate, patent-portfolio, and minute-book administration actions taken during the quarter—and related intellectual property records—are hereby ratified, confirmed, and approved as reflected in the Corporation’s books.



V. Adjournment:
There being no further business to come before the Board, the meeting was adjourned.

Derek E. Pappas
