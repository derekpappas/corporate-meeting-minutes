I. Call to Order:
The Special Meeting of the Board of Directors of Ritual Growth, Inc. (the “Corporation”) was called to order at 12:00 PM on 2025-12-09 by Derek E. Pappas, acting as Sole Director of the Corporation.

II. Roll Call and Quorum:
Director Present: Derek E. Pappas (Sole Director)

The Sole Director’s presence satisfied the quorum requirement, and the meeting proceeded as duly constituted under the Delaware General Corporation Law. The Sole Director confirmed that notice had been duly given or validly waived for this meeting.
The Sole Director attended using remote communications by which each participant could hear the others, and treated that participation as presence in person where permitted by applicable law and the Corporation’s bylaws.

III. Resolutions:
Upon consideration, the Sole Director adopted the following resolution:

Ratification of Product Delivery and Hosting Operations
RESOLVED, that engineering, hosting, and go-to-market decisions affecting the Corporation’s web applications and API services during 2025 are hereby ratified, confirmed, and approved in all respects.


The Sole Director stated that, in approving the matters minuted here, he relied in good faith on officer-prepared financial and operational materials and on other information, opinions, reports, and statements reasonably believed reliable on subjects within the presenters’ expert or professional competence, consistent with Section 141(e) of the Delaware General Corporation Law.


IV. Adjournment:
There being no further business to come before the Board, the meeting was adjourned.
