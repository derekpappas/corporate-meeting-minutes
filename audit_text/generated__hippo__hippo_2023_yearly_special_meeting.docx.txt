Hippo, Inc.
*(Board of Directors – DE corporation)*

Meeting Details
Date of Meeting: 2023-12-11
Time of Meeting: 12:00 PM
Location of Meeting: Thessaloniki, Greece, via digital communication
Address note: The principal address above is the Corporation’s designated notice and filing address on the corporate records. Purpose: Pre-annual review of marketplace, scraping, and data-pipeline operations

I. Call to Order:
The Special Meeting of the Board of Directors of Hippo, Inc. (the “Corporation”) was called to order at 12:00 PM on 2023-12-11 by Derek E. Pappas, acting as Sole Director of the Corporation.

II. Roll Call and Quorum:
Director Present: Derek E. Pappas (Sole Director)
Director Absent: None

The Sole Director confirmed that notice of the meeting was duly given or waived.
With Derek E. Pappas present as the Sole Director, the Board had a quorum and lawfully convened to transact business under the Delaware General Corporation Law.
The Sole Director attended using remote communications by which each participant could hear the others, and treated that participation as presence in person where permitted by applicable law and the Corporation’s bylaws.

III. Resolutions:
Upon consideration, the Sole Director adopted the following resolution:

Ratification of Marketplace and Data Operations
RESOLVED, that operational and engineering decisions affecting the Corporation’s consumer marketplace, merchant integrations, and data ingestion pipelines during 2023 are hereby ratified, confirmed, and approved in all respects.


For the actions described herein, the Sole Director relied in good faith on materials and oral presentations furnished for the meeting—including financial and operating summaries from officers of the Corporation—and on other information, reports, and statements presented as to matters within the presenters’ professional or expert competence, within the meaning of Section 141(e) of the Delaware General Corporation Law.


IV. Adjournment:
There being no further business to come before the Board, the meeting was adjourned.
