Operating addendum (Exhibit B)
Hippo, Inc.
(Delaware corporation)

Purpose
This addendum supplements the Minutes of the Annual Meeting of the Board of Directors of the Corporation for 2022 and is designated as Exhibit B for attachment to those minutes upon filing with the minute book.

Detailed accomplishments (2022)

- Led project management and coordinated development timelines
- Refactored and redesigned the application to improve performance and maintainability
- Implemented critical API updates aligned with product requirements
- Developed UI screens for products, collections, and deals
- Introduced social features including likes and comments
- Implemented CI/CD pipelines and testing infrastructure
- Enhanced scraping systems, improving data quality and coverage
- Decommissioned legacy Hadoop-based pipeline after infrastructure failure
- Avoided high-cost vendor lock-in and initiated transition to new data solutions

To the extent referenced in the minutes, this addendum may also include or incorporate by reference supplemental technical materials furnished for the meeting (including technical specifications, roadmaps, KPIs, and architecture diagrams).
