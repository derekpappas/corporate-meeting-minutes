II. Call to Order
The Annual Meeting of the Board of Directors of Ritual Growth, Inc. (the “Corporation”) was called to order at 1:00 PM on 2026-12-15 by Derek E. Pappas, acting as Sole Director, President, and Treasurer of the Corporation.

III. Roll Call and Quorum
Director Present:
Derek E. Pappas (Sole Director)

There were no additional directors and no absences. The Sole Director’s presence satisfied the quorum requirement, and the meeting proceeded as duly constituted under the Delaware General Corporation Law. The Sole Director confirmed that notice of the meeting was duly given or waived.
The Sole Director attended using remote communications by which each participant could hear the others, and treated that participation as presence in person where permitted by applicable law and the Corporation’s bylaws.

IV. Approval of Prior Minutes
The minutes of the prior Annual Meeting of the Board of Directors held on 2025-12-09 were reviewed and approved by the Sole Director.
V. Reports of Officers

President’s Report:
The Sole Director reported on the Corporation’s operational and engineering activities for the fiscal year, including centralized management of globally distributed development and the use of operational office location(s) during the fiscal year, with operations conducted from Wayne, Pennsylvania, USA and development from Poland; Romania; Portugal, while confirming that management, oversight, and decision-making remained centralized and continuously recorded through the Corporation’s official records. The Sole Director summarized continued product development during the year, including API servers and services and web applications; the Corporation did not ship a separate consumer mobile application during the period summarized.  The Corporation continued to operate hardware and cloud infrastructure using hosting providers including DigitalOcean and Hetzner. The President’s report included the following accomplishments summary for the calendar year 2026: Planning and build-out phase: system flow definition and initial data systems. A written addendum designated as Exhibit B for attachment to these minutes upon filing sets forth detailed accomplishments for the period covered by the President’s report, together with supplemental technical materials furnished for the meeting (including technical specifications, roadmaps, KPIs, and architecture diagrams) where applicable. All application code, APIs, and related intellectual property developed during the year were reaffirmed as the exclusive property of the Corporation.

Treasurer’s Report:
The Treasurer reported that the Corporation remains solvent and that certain outstanding obligations, including convertible instruments and vendor payment terms that remain contingent on a future liquidity event, the timing of which has not yet been determined. The Sole Director acknowledged the status of such obligations and confirmed continued oversight of these matters. Franchise taxes and registered agent fees are paid and current. The Corporation has 4,000,000 shares of common stock issued and outstanding at a par value of $.0001 per share.

For the actions described herein, the Sole Director relied in good faith on materials and oral presentations furnished for the meeting—including financial and operating summaries from officers of the Corporation—and on other information, reports, and statements presented as to matters within the presenters’ professional or expert competence, within the meaning of Section 141(e) of the Delaware General Corporation Law.


VI. Discussion Items
The Sole Director discussed go-to-market and client delivery priorities for 2027, including mobile companion workstreams, partner channel commitments, and release scheduling for the Corporation’s core web stack.

VII. Resolutions
Upon consideration, the Sole Director adopted the following resolutions:

RESOLVED, that the financial statements for the fiscal year 2026 are hereby approved.

Approval of 2027 Budget
RESOLVED, that the operating, engineering, and marketing budget for the fiscal year 2027 is hereby approved.

Stockholder Written Consent (2026)
The Sole Director noted that the Written Consent of Sole Stockholder dated December 15, 2026, adopting stockholder resolutions under DGCL §228 for the year 2026, will be filed with the minutes of the stockholders of the Corporation upon execution, to be designated Exhibit A upon filing.


VIII. Adjournment
There being no further business to come before the Board, the meeting was adjourned.
