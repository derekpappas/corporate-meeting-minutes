
Minutes of the Special Meeting of the Board of Directors - 2024
Loki Sports Enterprises, Inc.
*(Board of Directors – WY corporation)*

Meeting Details
Date of Meeting: 2024-12-14
Time of Meeting: 12:00 PM
Location of Meeting: Denver, NC, via digital communication
Address note: The principal address above is the Corporation’s designated notice and filing address on the corporate records. The Corporation is governed by the Wyoming Business Corporation Act. Purpose: Pre-annual review of sports media products and venue-facing integrations

I. Call to Order:
The Special Meeting of the Board of Directors of Loki Sports Enterprises, Inc. (the “Corporation”) was called to order at 12:00 PM on 2024-12-14 by Derek E. Pappas, acting as Sole Director of the Corporation.

II. Roll Call and Quorum:
Director Present: Derek E. Pappas (Sole Director)
Director Absent: None

The Sole Director being present, a quorum was present, and the meeting was duly constituted to transact business in accordance with the Wyoming Business Corporation Act. Notice for the meeting had been provided or waived as required, which the Sole Director confirmed for the record.
The Sole Director joined the meeting by approved digital means, with audio contemporaneous among participants, and such attendance was recorded as present in person at the meeting for quorum purposes.

III. Resolutions:
Upon consideration, the Sole Director adopted the following resolution:

Ratification of Sports Media and Venue Integration Work
RESOLVED, that engineering and commercial decisions affecting the Corporation’s sports media stack and venue-facing integrations during 2024 are hereby ratified, confirmed, and approved in all respects.


In taking the actions reflected in these minutes, the Sole Director relied in good faith on information, opinions, reports, and statements—including financial and operational materials prepared for this meeting and presentations from officers of the Corporation—as to matters the Sole Director reasonably believed were within such persons’ professional or expert competence, as contemplated by W.S. 1977 § 17-16-830 (Wyoming Business Corporation Act; standards for directors and reliance on information from officers and others reasonably believed reliable in their areas of competence).


IV. Adjournment:
There being no further business to come before the Board, the meeting was adjourned.
