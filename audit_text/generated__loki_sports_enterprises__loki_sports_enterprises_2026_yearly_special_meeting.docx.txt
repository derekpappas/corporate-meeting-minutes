
Minutes of the Special Meeting of the Board of Directors - 2026
Loki Sports Enterprises, Inc.
(Board of Directors – WY corporation)

Meeting Details
Date of Meeting: 2026-12-19
Time of Meeting: 12:00 PM
Location of Meeting: Wayne, Pennsylvania, via digital communication
Address note: The principal address above is the Corporation’s designated notice and filing address on the corporate records. The Corporation is governed by the Wyoming Business Corporation Act. Place identifies where the meeting was conducted for the stated date (including by approved remote means).
Purpose: Pre-annual review of sports media products and venue-facing integrations

I. Call to Order:
The Special Meeting of the Board of Directors of Loki Sports Enterprises, Inc. (the “Corporation”) was called to order at 12:00 PM on 2026-12-19 by Derek E. Pappas, acting as Sole Director of the Corporation.

II. Roll Call and Quorum:
Director Present: Derek E. Pappas (Sole Director)
Director Absent: None

Quorum being established by the attendance of the Sole Director, the meeting was duly organized for business under the Wyoming Business Corporation Act. The Sole Director acknowledged on the record that notice requirements were satisfied by proper notice or waiver.
Remote participation was used for the Sole Director’s attendance; the means employed allowed contemporaneous hearing among participants and were treated as satisfying any applicable in-person presence requirement.

III. Resolutions:
Upon consideration, the Sole Director adopted the following resolution:

Ratification of Sports Media and Venue Integration Work
RESOLVED, that engineering and commercial decisions affecting the Corporation’s sports media stack and venue-facing integrations during 2026 are hereby ratified, confirmed, and approved in all respects.


For the resolutions adopted here, the Sole Director relied in good faith on materials furnished for the meeting and on oral and written presentations from officers, in each case on subjects treated as within such persons’ professional or expert competence, as contemplated by W.S. 1977 § 17-16-830 (Wyoming Business Corporation Act).


IV. Adjournment:
There being no further business to come before the Board, the meeting was adjourned.

Derek E. Pappas
