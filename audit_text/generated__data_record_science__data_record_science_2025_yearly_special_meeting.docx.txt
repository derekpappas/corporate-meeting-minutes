
Minutes of the Special Meeting of the Board of Directors - 2025
DATA RECORD SCIENCE, INC.
(Board of Directors – DE corporation)

Meeting Details
Date of Meeting: 2025-11-30
Time of Meeting: 12:00 PM
Location of Meeting: Wayne, Pennsylvania, via digital communication
Address note: The principal address above is the Corporation’s designated notice and filing address on the corporate records. The Corporation is governed by the Delaware General Corporation Law; statutory references in these minutes follow that domicile. Place (and, where applicable, remote participation) reflects where the meeting was conducted for the stated date and may differ from the principal address.
Purpose: Pre-annual review of corporate records, patent portfolio status, and registered-agent compliance

I. Call to Order:
The Special Meeting of the Board of Directors of DATA RECORD SCIENCE, INC. (the “Corporation”) was called to order at 12:00 PM on 2025-11-30 by Derek E. Pappas, acting as Sole Director of the Corporation.

II. Roll Call and Quorum:
Director Present: Derek E. Pappas (Sole Director)
Director Absent: None

Quorum being established by the attendance of the Sole Director, the meeting was duly organized for business under the Delaware General Corporation Law.
Notice for the meeting had been provided or waived as required, which the Sole Director confirmed for the record.
The Sole Director participated via communications equipment by means of which all persons participating in the meeting could hear each other, and such participation constituted presence in person at the meeting.

III. Resolutions:
Upon consideration, the Sole Director adopted the following resolutions:

Ratification of Corporate and Patent-Portfolio Administration
RESOLVED, that corporate housekeeping, outside-counsel coordination, and patent-portfolio maintenance decisions affecting the Corporation during 2025 are hereby ratified, confirmed, and approved in all respects.

Record Date for Annual Meeting of Stockholders (DGCL §213)
RESOLVED, that 2025-11-30 is hereby fixed as the record date for determining the stockholders entitled to notice of and to vote at the Annual Meeting of Stockholders of the Corporation to be held on 2025-12-10 commencing at 1:00 PM, in accordance with the Corporation’s bylaws and the Delaware General Corporation Law.


The Sole Director stated that, in approving the matters minuted here, he relied in good faith on officer-prepared financial and operational materials and on other information, opinions, reports, and statements reasonably believed reliable on subjects within the presenters’ expert or professional competence, consistent with Section 141(e) of the Delaware General Corporation Law.


IV. Adjournment:
There being no further business to come before the Board, the meeting was adjourned.

Executed by: Derek E. Pappas
Date: 2025-11-30
