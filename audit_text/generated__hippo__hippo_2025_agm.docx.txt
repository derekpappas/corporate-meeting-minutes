II. Call to Order
The Annual Meeting of the Board of Directors of Hippo, Inc. (the “Corporation”) was called to order at 1:00 PM on 2025-12-08 by Derek E. Pappas, acting as Sole Director, President, and Treasurer of the Corporation.

III. Roll Call and Quorum
Director Present:
Derek E. Pappas (Sole Director)

Director Absent:
None

The Sole Director acknowledged on the record that notice requirements were satisfied by proper notice or waiver.
With Derek E. Pappas present as the Sole Director, the Board had a quorum and lawfully convened to transact business under the Delaware General Corporation Law.
The Sole Director joined the meeting by approved digital means, with audio contemporaneous among participants, and such attendance was recorded as present in person at the meeting for quorum purposes.

IV. Approval of Prior Minutes
The minutes of the prior Annual Meeting of the Board of Directors held on 2024-12-09 were reviewed and approved by the Sole Director.
V. Reports of Officers

President’s Report:
The Sole Director reported on the Corporation’s operational and engineering activities for the fiscal year, including centralized management of globally distributed development and the use of operational office location(s) during the fiscal year, with operations conducted from Denver, North Carolina, USA; Lantana, Florida, USA; Wayne, Pennsylvania, USA and development from Serbia; Bosnia and Herzegovina; Tunisia, while confirming that management, oversight, and decision-making remained centralized and continuously recorded through the Corporation’s official records. The Sole Director summarized continued development of the Corporation’s software and service offerings.  The President’s report included the following accomplishments summary for the calendar year 2025: Release and scaling phase: public launch, continued backend improvements, and scaling of data processing and search capabilities. A written addendum designated as Exhibit B for attachment to these minutes upon filing sets forth detailed accomplishments for the period covered by the President’s report, together with supplemental technical materials furnished for the meeting (including technical specifications, roadmaps, KPIs, and architecture diagrams) where applicable. All software, data models, algorithms, and related intellectual property developed during the year were reaffirmed as the exclusive property of the Corporation.

Treasurer’s Report:
The Treasurer reported that the Corporation remains solvent and that certain outstanding obligations, including notes payable and similar obligations that remain contingent on a future liquidity event, the timing of which has not yet been determined. The Sole Director acknowledged the status of such obligations and confirmed continued oversight of these matters. Franchise taxes and registered agent fees are paid and current. The Corporation has 8,160,000 shares of common stock issued and outstanding at a par value of $.0001 per share.

After reviewing the materials on file for the meeting, the Sole Director relied in good faith on information, opinions, reports, and statements—including presentations from officers of the Corporation—on matters reasonably believed to fall within such persons’ professional or expert competence, under Section 141(e) of the Delaware General Corporation Law.


VI. Discussion Items
The Sole Director discussed the Corporation’s product and data roadmap for 2026, including API reliability targets, merchant integration milestones, and fraud-prevention initiatives.

VII. Resolutions
Upon consideration, the Sole Director adopted the following resolutions:

Approval of Financial Reports
RESOLVED, that the financial statements for the fiscal year 2025 are hereby approved.

Approval of 2026 Budget
RESOLVED, that the operating, engineering, and marketing budget for the fiscal year 2026 is hereby approved.

Banking Authorization
RESOLVED, that Derek E. Pappas is authorized to open, maintain, and manage one or more corporate bank accounts in the name of the Corporation at JPMorgan Chase Bank, N.A., and any successor institution, and to act as the sole authorized signatory with full authority to execute all related documents.



Stockholder Written Consent (2025)
The Sole Director noted that the Written Consent of Sole Stockholder dated December 08, 2025, adopting stockholder resolutions under DGCL §228 for the year 2025, will be filed with the minutes of the stockholders of the Corporation upon execution, to be designated Exhibit A upon filing.


VIII. Adjournment
There being no further business to come before the Board, the meeting was adjourned.
