
Minutes of the Special Meeting of the Board of Directors - 2026
SurveyTeams, Inc.
(Board of Directors – DE corporation)

Meeting Details
Date of Meeting: 2026-12-08
Time of Meeting: 12:00 PM
Location of Meeting: Wayne, Pennsylvania, via digital communication
Address note: The principal address above is the Corporation’s designated notice and filing address on the corporate records. Purpose: Pre-annual review of survey platform operations and research compliance workflows

I. Call to Order:
The Special Meeting of the Board of Directors of SurveyTeams, Inc. (the “Corporation”) was called to order at 12:00 PM on 2026-12-08 by Derek E. Pappas, acting as Sole Director of the Corporation.

II. Roll Call and Quorum:
Director Present: Derek E. Pappas (Sole Director)
Director Absent: None

With Derek E. Pappas present as the Sole Director, the Board had a quorum and lawfully convened to transact business under the Delaware General Corporation Law.
Notice for the meeting had been provided or waived as required, which the Sole Director confirmed for the record.
Remote participation was used for the Sole Director’s attendance; the means employed allowed contemporaneous hearing among participants and were treated as satisfying any applicable in-person presence requirement.

III. Resolutions:
Upon consideration, the Sole Director adopted the following resolutions:

Ratification of Survey Platform and Research Operations
RESOLVED, that engineering and operations decisions affecting the Corporation’s survey tooling, sampling workflows, and research compliance posture during 2026 are hereby ratified, confirmed, and approved in all respects.

Record Date for Annual Meeting of Stockholders (DGCL §213)
RESOLVED, that 2026-12-08 is hereby fixed as the record date for determining the stockholders entitled to notice of and to vote at the Annual Meeting of Stockholders of the Corporation to be held on 2026-12-18 commencing at 1:00 PM, in accordance with the Corporation’s bylaws and the Delaware General Corporation Law.


After reviewing the materials on file for the meeting, the Sole Director relied in good faith on information, opinions, reports, and statements—including presentations from officers of the Corporation—on matters reasonably believed to fall within such persons’ professional or expert competence, under Section 141(e) of the Delaware General Corporation Law.


IV. Adjournment:
There being no further business to come before the Board, the meeting was adjourned.

Derek E. Pappas
