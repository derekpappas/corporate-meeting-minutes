
Minutes of the Quarterly Governance Meeting - 2023 Q1
Loki Sports Enterprises, Inc.
*(Board of Directors – WY corporation)*

Meeting Details
Date of Meeting: 2023-04-06
Time of Meeting: 1:00 PM
Location of Meeting: Wayne, Pennsylvania, via digital communication
Address note: The principal address above is the Corporation’s designated notice and filing address on the corporate records. The Corporation is governed by the Wyoming Business Corporation Act. I. Call to Order:
The Quarterly Governance Meeting of the Board of Directors of Loki Sports Enterprises, Inc. (the “Corporation”) was called to order at 1:00 PM on 2023-04-06 by Derek E. Pappas, acting as Sole Director of the Corporation.

II. Roll Call and Quorum:
Director Present: Derek E. Pappas (Sole Director)
Director Absent: None

Quorum being established by the attendance of the Sole Director, the meeting was duly organized for business under the Wyoming Business Corporation Act. Notice for the meeting had been provided or waived as required, which the Sole Director confirmed for the record.
The Sole Director joined the meeting by approved digital means, with audio contemporaneous among participants, and such attendance was recorded as present in person at the meeting for quorum purposes.

III. Business Review:
The Sole Director reviewed quarterly infrastructure stability and confirmed that all assets, including software and related intellectual property, created during the quarter in the development centers located in United States; Pakistan are properly titled to and are the exclusive property of the Corporation.

For the resolutions adopted here, the Sole Director relied in good faith on materials furnished for the meeting and on oral and written presentations from officers, in each case on subjects treated as within such persons’ professional or expert competence, as contemplated by W.S. 1977 § 17-16-830 (Wyoming Business Corporation Act).


IV. Resolution:
Upon consideration, the Sole Director adopted the following resolution:

RESOLVED, that all sports-media, venue-integration, and supporting infrastructure work completed during the quarter—and related intellectual property—is hereby ratified, confirmed, and approved as assets of the Corporation.



V. Adjournment:
There being no further business to come before the Board, the meeting was adjourned.
