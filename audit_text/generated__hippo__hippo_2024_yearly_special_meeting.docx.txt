
Minutes of the Special Meeting of the Board of Directors - 2024
Hippo, Inc.
(Board of Directors – DE corporation)

Meeting Details
Date of Meeting: 2024-12-09
Time of Meeting: 12:00 PM
Location of Meeting: Denver, NC, via digital communication
Address note: The principal address above is the Corporation’s designated notice and filing address on the corporate records. The Corporation is governed by the Delaware General Corporation Law; statutory references in these minutes follow that domicile. Place (and, where applicable, remote participation) reflects where the meeting was conducted for the stated date and may differ from the principal address.
Purpose: Pre-annual review of marketplace, scraping, and data-pipeline operations

I. Call to Order:
The Special Meeting of the Board of Directors of Hippo, Inc. (the “Corporation”) was called to order at 12:00 PM on 2024-12-09 by Derek E. Pappas, acting as Sole Director of the Corporation.

II. Roll Call and Quorum:
Director Present: Derek E. Pappas (Sole Director)
Director Absent: None

The Sole Director acknowledged on the record that notice requirements were satisfied by proper notice or waiver.
The Sole Director’s presence satisfied the quorum requirement, and the meeting proceeded as duly constituted under the Delaware General Corporation Law.
The Sole Director joined the meeting by approved digital means, with audio contemporaneous among participants, and such attendance was recorded as present in person at the meeting for quorum purposes.

III. Resolutions:
Upon consideration, the Sole Director adopted the following resolution:

Ratification of Marketplace and Data Operations
RESOLVED, that operational and engineering decisions affecting the Corporation’s consumer marketplace, merchant integrations, and data ingestion pipelines during 2024 are hereby ratified, confirmed, and approved in all respects.


The Sole Director stated that, in approving the matters minuted here, he relied in good faith on officer-prepared financial and operational materials and on other information, opinions, reports, and statements reasonably believed reliable on subjects within the presenters’ expert or professional competence, consistent with Section 141(e) of the Delaware General Corporation Law.


IV. Adjournment:
There being no further business to come before the Board, the meeting was adjourned.

Derek E. Pappas
