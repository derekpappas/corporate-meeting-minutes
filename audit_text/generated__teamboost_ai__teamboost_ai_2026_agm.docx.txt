II. Call to Order
The Annual Meeting of the Board of Directors of TeamBoost.ai, Inc. (the “Corporation”) was called to order at 1:00 PM on 2026-12-17 by Derek E. Pappas, acting as Sole Director, President, and Treasurer of the Corporation.

IV. Approval of Prior Minutes
The minutes of the prior Annual Meeting of the Board of Directors held on 2025-12-11 were reviewed and approved by the Sole Director.
V. Reports of Officers

President’s Report:
The Sole Director reported on the Corporation’s operational and engineering activities for the fiscal year, including centralized management of globally distributed development and the use of operational office location(s) during the fiscal year, with operations conducted from Wayne, Pennsylvania, USA and development from Brazil; Nigeria; Portugal, while confirming that management, oversight, and decision-making remained centralized and continuously recorded through the Corporation’s official records. The Sole Director summarized continued product development during the year, including API servers and services, web applications, and mobile applications.  The Corporation continued to operate hardware and cloud infrastructure using hosting providers including DigitalOcean and Hetzner. A written addendum designated as Exhibit B for attachment to these minutes upon filing sets forth additional detail furnished for the meeting, including technical specifications, roadmaps, KPIs, and architecture diagrams. All mobile clients, server-side services, and related intellectual property developed during the year were reaffirmed as the exclusive property of the Corporation.

Treasurer’s Report:
The Treasurer reported that the Corporation remains solvent and that certain outstanding obligations, including SAFEs, convertible notes, and similar instruments that remain contingent on a future liquidity event, the timing of which has not yet been determined. The Sole Director acknowledged the status of such obligations and confirmed continued oversight of these matters. Franchise taxes and registered agent fees are paid and current. The Corporation has 10,000,000 shares of common stock issued and outstanding at a par value of $.0001 per share.

The Sole Director recorded reliance, in good faith, on the financial and operational materials circulated or reviewed for this meeting and on statements from officers of the Corporation on matters reasonably treated as within such persons’ professional or expert competence, as permitted by Section 141(e) of the Delaware General Corporation Law.


VI. Discussion Items
The Sole Director discussed mobile product velocity and infrastructure scaling for 2027, including app-store release cadence, on-device performance budgets, and push-notification reliability targets.
