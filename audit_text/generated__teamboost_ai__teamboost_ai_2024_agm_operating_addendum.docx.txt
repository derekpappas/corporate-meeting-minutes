Operating addendum (Exhibit B)
TeamBoost.ai, Inc.
*(Delaware corporation)*

Purpose
This addendum supplements the Minutes of the Annual Meeting of the Board of Directors of the Corporation for 2024 and is annexed to those minutes as Exhibit B.

Detailed accomplishments (2024)

- Continued development of the Teamboost mobile and web applications
- Implemented key features including Questions, Releases, Insights, and Daily Reports
- Added internationalization support for multiple languages and regional formats
- Designed and built an in-app reporting system for user feedback
- Created and maintained technical specifications for the Teamboost API
- Enhanced onboarding, organization management, and notifications services
- Expanded backend with services including DMS, reminders, EWS, requests/questions, comments, and attachments
- Improved backend scalability for communication, collaboration, and content management
- Enhanced the tasks service to support new features and evolving requirements
- Performed testing, product ideation, and backend operational support

Other materials
To the extent referenced in the minutes, this addendum may also include or incorporate by reference supplemental technical materials furnished for the meeting (including technical specifications, roadmaps, KPIs, and architecture diagrams).
