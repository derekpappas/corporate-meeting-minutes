II. Call to Order
The Annual Meeting of the Board of Directors of Hippo, Inc. (the “Corporation”) was called to order at 1:00 PM on 2024-12-09 by Derek E. Pappas, acting as Sole Director, President, and Treasurer of the Corporation.

III. Roll Call and Quorum
Director Present:
Derek E. Pappas (Sole Director)

Director Absent:
None

The Sole Director confirmed that notice of the meeting was duly given or waived.
With Derek E. Pappas present as the Sole Director, the Board had a quorum and lawfully convened to transact business under the Delaware General Corporation Law.
Remote participation was used for the Sole Director’s attendance; the means employed allowed contemporaneous hearing among participants and were treated as satisfying any applicable in-person presence requirement.

IV. Approval of Prior Minutes
The minutes of the prior Annual Meeting of the Board of Directors held on 2023-12-11 were reviewed and approved by the Sole Director.
V. Reports of Officers

President’s Report:
The Sole Director reported on the Corporation’s operational and engineering activities for the fiscal year, including centralized management of globally distributed development and the use of operational office location(s) during the fiscal year, with operations conducted from Thessaloniki, Greece; Valencia, Spain; Knoxville, TN; Belgrade, Serbia; Athens, Greece; Istanbul, Turkey; Birmingham, England; Hoegaarden, Belgium; Denver, North Carolina, USA and development from Serbia; Bosnia and Herzegovina; Tunisia, while confirming that management, oversight, and decision-making remained centralized and continuously recorded through the Corporation’s official records. The Sole Director summarized continued development of the Corporation’s software and service offerings.  The President’s report included the following accomplishments summary for the calendar year 2024: Rebuild and optimization phase: full system redesign, infrastructure simplification, and significant cost reduction with improved scalability. A written addendum designated as Exhibit B for attachment to these minutes upon filing sets forth detailed accomplishments for the period covered by the President’s report, together with supplemental technical materials furnished for the meeting (including technical specifications, roadmaps, KPIs, and architecture diagrams) where applicable. All software, data models, algorithms, and related intellectual property developed during the year were reaffirmed as the exclusive property of the Corporation.

Treasurer’s Report:
The Treasurer reported that the Corporation remains solvent and that certain outstanding obligations, including notes payable and similar obligations that remain contingent on a future liquidity event, the timing of which has not yet been determined. The Sole Director acknowledged the status of such obligations and confirmed continued oversight of these matters. Franchise taxes and registered agent fees are paid and current. The Corporation has 8,160,000 shares of common stock issued and outstanding at a par value of $.0001 per share.

The Sole Director recorded reliance, in good faith, on the financial and operational materials circulated or reviewed for this meeting and on statements from officers of the Corporation on matters reasonably treated as within such persons’ professional or expert competence, as permitted by Section 141(e) of the Delaware General Corporation Law.


VI. Discussion Items
The Sole Director discussed the Corporation’s product and data roadmap for 2025, including API reliability targets, merchant integration milestones, and fraud-prevention initiatives.

VII. Resolutions
Upon consideration, the Sole Director adopted the following resolutions:

Approval of Financial Reports
RESOLVED, that the financial statements for the fiscal year 2024 are hereby approved.

Approval of 2025 Budget
RESOLVED, that the operating, engineering, and marketing budget for the fiscal year 2025 is hereby approved.

Banking Authorization
RESOLVED, that Derek E. Pappas is authorized to open, maintain, and manage one or more corporate bank accounts in the name of the Corporation at JPMorgan Chase Bank, N.A., and any successor institution, and to act as the sole authorized signatory with full authority to execute all related documents.



Stockholder Written Consent (2024)
The Sole Director noted that the Written Consent of Sole Stockholder dated December 09, 2024, adopting stockholder resolutions under DGCL §228 for the year 2024, will be filed with the minutes of the stockholders of the Corporation upon execution, to be designated Exhibit A upon filing.


VIII. Adjournment
There being no further business to come before the Board, the meeting was adjourned.
