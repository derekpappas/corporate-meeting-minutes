II. Call to Order
The Annual Meeting of the Board of Directors of Loki Sports Enterprises, Inc. (the “Corporation”) was called to order at 1:00 PM on 2025-12-13 by Derek E. Pappas, acting as Sole Director, President, and Treasurer of the Corporation.

III. Roll Call and Quorum
Director Present:
Derek E. Pappas (Sole Director)

Director Absent:
None

The Sole Director being present, a quorum was present, and the meeting was duly constituted to transact business in accordance with the Wyoming Business Corporation Act. The Sole Director acknowledged on the record that notice requirements were satisfied by proper notice or waiver.
The Sole Director participated via communications equipment by means of which all persons participating in the meeting could hear each other, and such participation constituted presence in person at the meeting.

IV. Approval of Prior Minutes
The minutes of the prior Annual Meeting of the Board of Directors held on 2024-12-14 were reviewed and approved by the Sole Director.
V. Reports of Officers

President’s Report:
The Sole Director reported on the Corporation’s operational and engineering activities for the fiscal year, including centralized management of globally distributed development and the use of operational office location(s) during the fiscal year, with operations conducted from Denver, North Carolina, USA; Lantana, Florida, USA; Wayne, Pennsylvania, USA and development from United States; Pakistan, while confirming that management, oversight, and decision-making remained centralized and continuously recorded through the Corporation’s official records. The Sole Director summarized sports-media and venue-integration work during the year, including coordination with domestic (United States) operations and hockey stick manufacturing run through Pakistan-based production partners, with inventory and quality oversight centralized through the Corporation.  All sports-media software, venue-integration tooling, product designs, manufacturing specifications for hockey sticks produced through the Corporation’s supply chain, and related intellectual property developed during the year were reaffirmed as the exclusive property of the Corporation.

Treasurer’s Report:
The Treasurer reported that the Corporation remains solvent and that certain outstanding obligations, including equipment financing and season-cycle receivables that remain contingent on a future liquidity event, the timing of which has not yet been determined. The Sole Director acknowledged the status of such obligations and confirmed continued oversight of these matters. Franchise taxes and registered agent fees are paid and current. The Corporation has 10,000,000 shares of common stock issued and outstanding at a par value of $.0001 per share.

In taking the actions reflected in these minutes, the Sole Director relied in good faith on information, opinions, reports, and statements—including financial and operational materials prepared for this meeting and presentations from officers of the Corporation—as to matters the Sole Director reasonably believed were within such persons’ professional or expert competence, as contemplated by W.S. 1977 § 17-16-830 (Wyoming Business Corporation Act; standards for directors and reliance on information from officers and others reasonably believed reliable in their areas of competence).


VI. Discussion Items
The Sole Director discussed fan engagement, venue partnerships, and media integrations for 2026, including tournament-season logistics, rights-holder coordination, and in-venue mobile experiences.

VII. Resolutions
Upon consideration, the Sole Director adopted the following resolutions:

Approval of Financial Reports
RESOLVED, that the financial statements for the fiscal year 2025 are hereby approved.

Approval of 2026 Budget
RESOLVED, that the operating, engineering, and marketing budget for the fiscal year 2026 is hereby approved.

Banking Authorization
RESOLVED, that Derek E. Pappas is authorized to open, maintain, and manage one or more corporate bank accounts in the name of the Corporation at Truist Bank, and any successor institution, and to act as the sole authorized signatory with full authority to execute all related documents.



Stockholder Written Consent (2025)
The Sole Director noted that the Written Consent of Sole Stockholder dated December 13, 2025, adopting stockholder resolutions under W.S. 1977 § 17-16-704 for the year 2025, will be filed with the minutes of the stockholders of the Corporation upon execution, to be designated Exhibit A upon filing.


VIII. Adjournment
There being no further business to come before the Board, the meeting was adjourned.

Executed and agreed:
Derek E. Pappas
