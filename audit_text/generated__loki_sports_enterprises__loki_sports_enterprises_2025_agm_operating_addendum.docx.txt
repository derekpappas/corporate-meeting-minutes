Operating addendum (Exhibit B)
Loki Sports Enterprises, Inc.
(Wyoming corporation)

Purpose
This addendum supplements the Minutes of the Annual Meeting of the Board of Directors of the Corporation for 2025 and is designated as Exhibit B for attachment to those minutes upon filing with the minute book.

Detailed accomplishments (2025)

- Formalized partnerships with new entities in Berlin, Germany, and Pretoria, South Africa
- Diversified the supply chain by appointing a new lead agent in Sialkot and onboarding AliTra as an additional manufacturing factory in Sialkot
- Launched commercial distribution of AliTra sticks in the New York market
- Expanded transatlantic logistics with significant shipments to Berlin, including AliTra indoor and outdoor sticks and Malik junior outdoor sticks
